About Aimable EXPRESS LTD

Launched on 20 June 2025 in Huye District Southern Province of

Rwanda, Aimable Express Ltd is a registered Public Transport

Company based in Kigali with branches in Huye, Muhanga, Ruhango,

Nyanza, Gisagara, Nyamagabe, and Nyaruguru,musanze,nyagatare.

We are the first Public Transport Company to operate in all

regions of Rwanda country.

With our young fleet of vehicles, we serve the public with high-quality

services and comfort linking clients to all destinations in Rwanda

Country. As we grow day to day, our services are no longer Public

Transport only, we also have Courier and Parcels Delivery Services

and special training about Transport operating nationwide through

our company Aimable Ltd.
